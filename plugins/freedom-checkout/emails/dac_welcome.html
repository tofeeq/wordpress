"Bird-Dogging" or "Hand-Holding" the Loan Package?

You can either bird-dog prospective clients to DAC website, or you can better care for the client by hand-holding and retrieve the loan application/docs yourself.

If bird-dogging, you simply refer them to the DAC website and have them complete the pre-qualification form on the main page.  When they do meet our minimum qualifications, our inside client support sales team will reach out to the client to help answer questions and gather the loan application/documents.  If they don't surpass our minimums, we would notify them of such.

You'll be notified via email of any prequalification requests at the time we receive them and complete the findings.  We encourage you to congratulate them and encourage they respond to our communication and document requests.

If hand-holding, you prequalify the prospects during your conversations (according to qualifications discussed in training) and then provide them with the application and list of needed documents.    You collect those documents via emailed PDF and forward them to our offices (or have them forward directly).

Hand-holding is most effective and generates ongoing leads from your clients.  We recommend this approach but support bird-dogging for those who simply want to refer a person now and then, or while you are getting your feet wet.

When you submit a loan package, simply email the application and supporting documents to submissions@davidallencapital.com.  You will need to submit:

  1.     Paper Application - Completely filled out
  2.     3 Months Bank Account Statements (all pages)
  3.     3 Months Merchant Processing Statements (if they take credit cards)
  4.     Voided Check
  5.     Drivers License
  6.     2015 Business Taxes (all pages)
